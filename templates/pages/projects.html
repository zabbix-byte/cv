AI Procurement Platform · Valerdat

The product I designed and built as founding engineer and CTO: an MRP platform that turns ERP supply-chain data into demand forecasts and purchase proposals planners can trust.

Scale is a property of the whole system, not one layer — today it runs across 500k+ SKUs and 10TB+ of data, with forecasting below 5% MAPE using Prophet, XGBoost, TFT, and thirteen other models (best fit per SKU and warehouse). An optimizer turns those forecasts into buy proposals; LLMs help produce orders ready to validate.

Underneath that product surface sits the platform that makes it possible: multi-tenant SaaS with isolated data and RBAC, bidirectional connectors into Sage 200, Dynamics 365 Business Central, SAP and others (API/SFTP — also on the Sage Marketplace), and Databricks/Spark pipelines that ingest, clean, and prepare each client’s data before forecasting.

For how the pieces connect — ingest → forecast → purchase → product, plus the agent layer — see the architecture walkthrough under Experience.
