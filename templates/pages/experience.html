Architecture

How demand prediction works in the product: ERP data in, purchase proposals out — and agents on top so people can work with the results in natural language.

From ERP data to purchase proposals

→ → →

Select a stage to follow the path from data to purchases.

  1. Ingest. Connectors pull from SAP, Dynamics 365 Business Central, and Sage into S3. Databricks jobs validate the data, apply each client’s rules, and prepare features for forecasting — about 1.5 hours per client.
  2. Forecast. Thirteen models compete; we keep the best fit per SKU and warehouse instead of one global model for everything.
  3. Purchase. An optimizer turns those forecasts into purchase proposals under each customer’s business constraints.
  4. Product. Results land in PostgreSQL and surface in the Angular app for review and confirmation.
  5. Agents. A layer on top of the whole stack lets users query demand and proposals in natural language, not only through the classic UI.
